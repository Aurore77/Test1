Enter the empire of KARUVAM! An epic adventure of love and conflict where natural elements are raging.

THE STORY

One night, counting his richness, Gevurak, conqueststhisrty King, realizes his land is too small for his magnificent greatness.
The King suddently decides to invade Karuvam the peaceful land of water with his army.
Sent to investigate and spy on Marukamers' habits, General Fritran meets Princess Melindra on a fair in the city center. Her charm works beautifully on the General.
After spending some time together, they fall in love and reveal each other who they really are.
The conquest will not be as easy as usual for Gevurak this time...

THE CENTRAL CHARACTERS

  • Gevurak

    Descendant of a long lineage of warriors, Gevurak reigns on Marukam. As a king, he is a conqueror and he will not stop until his kingdom is as spread as his ego. To push the enemy surrender, Gevurak uses the power of fire.

  • Fritran

    General Fritan is a half-god who controls the element of fire. His mother is Mesembria, one of the daughters the Sun had with the Moon.
    Despite the fact that he is part of Gevurak's army and is following his King's orders, Fritran is a good man with feelings.

  • Melindra

    Princess Melindra and her people are amphibious and live in a land where water is a wealth. This romantic woman living in a blue world is about to meet the passion of fire.

  • Bellimon

    Melindra's brother, Bellimon is a young explorer who travelled the world for a few years and discovered other cultures. Thanks to his journeys abroad, he will reveal himself as a precious weapon to counterattack the assailant.

WHO ARE WE ?

OUR HISTORY

How to define us

"People might think we are a bunch of crazy people under a mystical influence and they would be right ! We reinvent our profession throughout shows where Mother Nature is our biggest source of inspiration."

Tom Filenhow, Wild Circus Manager.

The Wild team birth

In the early 1990s, a troup of performers coming from South Africa landed in the harbour of Brighton in England. Juggling, fire-breathing and playing mystical music, they started to delight locals and visitors on Brighton Pier.
Soon, they draw the attention of Max Dungleberg, one the most important Show Producers in the United Kingdom. M. Dungleberg, decided to offer them to ensure the first part of the Blackpool Tower Circus show. Since in 1990, the Blackpool Tower Circus stopped performing with animals, they were looking for a new concept.
In six months, Wild Circus company, began to get well known.
After sharing some of their knowledges and ideas with the Blackpool Tower company, Wild Circus troup decided to create its own first show. M. Dungleberg still charmed by the beauty of the mystical performances executed by Wild Circus, decided he would fund this first show "Magical Chikera". And that's how the story begun.

To be continued... With you

Now in its third decade, Wild Circus troup has evolved and grown over the years, in numbers and in spirit. Nowadays, Wild Circus is famous worldwide but the essence of what we created stays the same: provoke your senses and invoke your imagination reminding you we are all children of Mother of Nature.

THE ARTISTS

Paticha Erivols was born in Mongolia. She started contorsion when she was 8 years old and practiced synchronized swimming for 13 years. After that, Paticha, became a professional artist and went to perfom in Asia for 10 years.
She joined Wild Circus team in 2009. Paticha's intergration into the shows went smoothly and didn't take very long. Espacially since, she is a real professional artist.
After 5 years in the team, she became one of the main characters in KARUVAM.

Paulo Pendaror is part a third generation circus perfomers from Brasil. His primary activity is Hand to Hand with his brother Marcus. They entered the Wild Circus together in 2001.
Lately, Paulo and Marcus created a show involving lassos on fire. The Company enjoyed it and decided to add their performance to the new show Karuvam.

Miro Lacasse was born in France. His mother was an acrobate and his father a juggler. He started juggling at 5 years old and fire-breathing at 14 years old.
After several years working in fairs with his family, Miro decided to join our circus in 2005 as a juggler.
After six more years practicing fire-breathing, Miro decided to improve his acts by adding fire in them.

Andy Mac Nigall hails from the United-States of America. His domain a various but two of them are most important : aerial silk and juggling.
In 2013, after 10 years teaching the art of aerial silk in a Circus School, Andy was recruted by Wild Circus. Soon, a new show was created, and Andy has been offered the opportunity to be the King of Marukam: Gevurak in it.
