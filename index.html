KARUVAM

Enter the empire of KARUVAM! An epic adventure of love and conflict where natural elements are raging.

Gevurak rising
Follow Gevurak of Marukam in his conquest of a new land: Karuvam. Discover his burning passion throughout a fire act on the water that you will never forget.

Karuvam besieged
Meet Fritran of Marukam, the fire-eater and heroe. The way he controls fire is breathtaking !

The bounce of foes
Marukamers will not let Gevurak and his troups take their land ! Bellimon, the Prince, and his army will tame you as they do with fire.

Storm in a water bowl
Like Fritran, let yourself mesmerized by Melindra's swirl. Get ready for the emotional storm.
